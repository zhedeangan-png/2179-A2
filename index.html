Which lines carry the most people?

Access also depends on waiting time

Headway means the time between vehicles. A lower headway usually means a shorter wait for passengers.

BRT Sunway as a local connector

BRT Sunway is smaller than the main rail lines, but it plays a focused role around Sunway and USJ.

Final takeaway

The Klang Valley network is not experienced evenly. Access depends on where stations are located, how heavily lines are used, and how long passengers wait.
